The Flue ecosystem connects applications to external provider channels and
sandbox environments.

## Channels

Channel packages receive, verify, and normalize provider webhooks before your
application handles them. Start with [Channels](/docs/ecosystem/channels/) to
choose a provider and connect its events to agents, workflows, or
application-owned routes.

## Sandbox connectors

A sandbox connector adapts an application-owned compute or workspace
environment into the Flue sandbox contract. Choose a provider from the
**Sandboxes** navigation group, or read [Sandboxes](/docs/guide/sandboxes/) to
choose an execution boundary first.
